Line must meet all of the below criteria per COGCC’s 1105.c.(1) Abandonment in place regulations:
  • Flowline will be purged of any liquids, and
  • Depleted to atmospheric pressure, and
  • Risers cut to 3’ below grade or to the depth of the flowline (whichever is shallower), and
  • Ends of the flowline are sealed below grade, and
  • Above ground cathodic protection and equipment associated with the riser are removed
